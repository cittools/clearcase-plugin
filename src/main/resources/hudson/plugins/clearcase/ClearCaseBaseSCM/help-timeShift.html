Normally, all computers that use ClearCase MUST be synchronized on the same time server.

If your infrastructure does not allow you this, you can specify here the time delta in seconds between Hudson/Jenkins master computer and your VOBs server.

Be carefull, if your infrastructure has VOBs on several servers, this "time shift" will be used without distinction on all of them. This is why it is better to synchronize all the computers on the same reference.
